Frequently Asked Questions

What should I do that u talk?

Please bring your ID, insurance card, list of current medications, medical records (if available), and any relevant test results. Arriving 15 minutes early to complete paperwork is recommended.

How do I schedule an emergency appointment?

For medical emergencies, please call our emergency line at (555) 987-6543. We reserve slots each day for urgent cases and will accommodate you as quickly as possible.

Do you provide any lifeline?

Yes, we provide prescription refills for existing patients. Please contact us 3-5 days before you run out of medication. You can request refills through our patient portal or by calling our office.

What is your cancellation policy?

We request at least 24 hours notice for appointment cancellations. Late cancellations or no-shows may incur a fee. We understand emergencies happen, so please contact us as soon as possible if you need to reschedule.

Do you offer weekend calls?

We offer limited appointments on Saturday mornings from 9:00 AM to 1:00 PM. These slots fill quickly, so we recommend booking well in advance for weekend appointments.

How can I access my medical records?

Patients can access their medical records through our secure patient portal. For full medical record requests, please complete a release form available at our reception or downloadable from our website.
